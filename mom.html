The Soul of Kindness,

She is the first light that greets the morning,
The steady hand that guides without demanding.
Through every stumble and every triumph,
Her love remains—a constant, quiet understanding.

No measure can weigh the depth of her sacrifices,
No words can capture the warmth of her embrace.
She gives without keeping count,
Loves without condition,
And becomes the foundation upon which dreams are built..

Her patience is an unending river,
Her wisdom, the compass in life’s storms.
She is shelter. She is strength.
The keeper of memories, the weaver of futures.

On this day and all days,
She is celebrated—not just as a mother,
But as the extraordinary woman she is.
A force of nature wrapped in tenderness,
A love that shapes worlds
